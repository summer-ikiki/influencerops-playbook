Influencer Marketing Operating Playbook

红人营销流程手册

A practical workflow for sourcing, evaluating, contracting, launching, tracking, and renewing creators across influencer marketing programs.

这套流程用于梳理红人营销项目中的达人筛选、建联、签约、上线、数据追踪与长期合作。

01

North Star And Funnel

核心目标与增长漏斗

This playbook outlines a transferable influencer marketing workflow. Creator criteria, CPM targets, conversion metrics, and renewal standards should be adapted to each product, platform, and audience.

本手册梳理的是一套可迁移的红人营销流程。达人筛选标准、CPM 目标、转化指标和续约标准，都需要根据具体产品、平台、人群和增长目标调整。

Target Audience 目标受众 Target audiences, niche communities, creator-led trust 核心是通过合适的创作者，在目标人群中建立真实信任。
Creator Quality 达人质量 View median, community depth, comment quality, posting cadence 不只看粉丝量，更看近期播放、社群相关度、评论质量和更新稳定性。
Cost Efficiency 成本效率 Estimated CPM before deal, actual CPM after launch 合作前用预估 CPM 控制报价，上线后用实际 CPM 复盘投放效率。
Business Outcome 业务结果 Signups, conversion quality, reusable creator partnerships 最终关注注册、转化质量，以及能否沉淀为可复用的达人资产。

02

Use Case Scope

适用场景说明

The operating logic is transferable across products and platforms, but the scoring model should always be adapted to the product's audience, conversion path, and business goal.

这套流程逻辑可以迁移到不同产品和平台，但评分模型不能照搬，必须结合产品的人群、转化路径和业务目标重新设定。

Example Context 案例场景

Creator collaboration, audience-fit evaluation, creator-led trust.

重点是评估达人与目标人群的匹配度，并通过内容建立信任。

Transferable Workflow 可迁移流程

Sourcing, evaluation, outreach, approval, contract, content review, tracking, and renewal.

达人来源、评估、建联、审批、签约、内容审核、数据追踪和续约判断可以迁移。

Product-Specific Variables 需按产品调整的变量

Audience profile, creator archetype, CPM target, conversion event, content angle, and renewal threshold.

目标人群、达人类型、CPM 目标、转化事件、内容角度和续约门槛都需要按产品调整。

03

Creator Evaluation Scorecard

达人评估打分卡

Follower count is a weak first gate. The stronger question is whether the creator can repeatedly reach the right audience at an efficient cost.

粉丝量不是最可靠的第一筛选标准。更关键的问题是：这个达人能否以合理成本持续触达正确的人群。

30% 14-day view median 14 天播放中位数

Use recent median views to reduce volatility. For example, an Instagram creator collaboration may use 50k median views as an initial consideration threshold, then adjust by product and audience.

用近期播放中位数过滤偶然爆款。具体门槛要按平台和产品调整；例如以 Instagram 博主合作为例，可以先用 5 万中位播放作为进入初步考虑池的参考门槛。

20% Audience signal 人群/社群信号

Clear audience context, community relevance, niche authority, or strong signals from the target segment.

看内容里是否有明确的人群语境、社群相关性、垂类影响力，或来自目标用户的真实互动信号。

15% Comment quality 评论质量

Look for real target-audience comments, community references, and conversation quality over vanity engagement.

重点看评论里有没有真实目标用户、社群语境和有效讨论，而不是只看点赞数。

15% Content fit 内容匹配度

Prioritize vlogs, POVs, community stories, lifestyle narratives, and authentic on-camera presence.

优先选择 vlog、POV、社群故事和生活方式内容，创作者本人需要有真实、自然的表达能力。

10% Posting cadence 发布频率

Recent consistency matters because stale creators are harder to forecast and activate.

近期是否稳定更新很重要。长期不活跃的达人更难预测表现，也更难推进合作。

10% Estimated CPM 预估 CPM

Formula: proposed price / view median * 1,000. Use a product-specific benchmark; for example, some creator programs may start with a target such as below $20, then adjust by platform, category, and conversion value.

公式为报价 / 播放中位数 * 1000。CPM 目标应作为参考 benchmark，而不是固定标准；比如某些合作可以先以 20 美元以下作为参考，再根据平台、行业、账号质量和转化价值调整。

04

Operating Principles

操作原则

These principles keep influencer marketing disciplined, measurable, and scalable across campaigns.

这些原则用于保证红人营销不是靠感觉执行，而是可衡量、可复盘、可规模化地推进。

01 Quality over follower count 质量优先于粉丝量

Prioritize recent view performance, content fit, audience signal, and real comment quality.

粉丝量只能作为参考，更重要的是近期播放表现、内容是否匹配、人群信号是否明显、评论是否真实。

02 CPM before commitment 确认合作前先算 CPM

Use expected CPM to guide negotiation before a creator enters approval or contract stage.

在进入审批或签约前，先用预估 CPM 判断报价是否合理，让议价有数据依据。

03 Script before launch 上线前必须有脚本

Require a script or concept review to protect accuracy while preserving creator authenticity.

上线前必须看脚本或内容概念，既要保证品牌表达准确，也要保留创作者自己的语气。

04 Post link as source of truth 发布链接作为数据源

Once content goes live, the post link triggers tracking, reporting, and campaign learnings.

内容上线后，以发布链接作为统一追踪入口，用来抓取数据、生成报告和沉淀复盘经验。

05 Renewal is a performance decision 续约由表现决定

Long-term partnerships should be based on actual CPM, signups, view achievement, and comment quality.

是否续约不凭感觉，而要看实际 CPM、注册表现、播放达成率和评论质量。

05

决策门槛与质量控制

Clear gates reduce wasted outreach, inconsistent pricing, delayed content review, and weak post-campaign learnings.

明确的决策门槛可以减少无效建联、报价混乱、内容审核延误和复盘沉淀不足的问题。

06

Workflow Map

完整流程地图

Each stage has a clear owner, decision gate, and data artifact. This makes the process scalable across teammates.

每个阶段都需要明确负责人、决策门槛和沉淀数据资产，这样流程才能跨团队复制和规模化。

1 Source 达人来源

Find creators from TikTok, Instagram, creator marketplaces, niche communities, and competitor mentions.

从 TikTok、Instagram、达人平台、垂类社群和竞品提及中寻找候选达人。

2 Screen 初筛

Evaluate view median, audience signal, comments, cadence, and content style before contacting.

建联前先评估播放中位数、人群信号、评论质量、发布频率和内容风格。

3 Duplicate Check 重复检查

Search the creator CRM and internal network before outreach to avoid messy double-contact.

在 CRM 和内部人脉中检查是否已联系，避免多人重复触达同一达人。

4 Outreach 建联

Send personalized email or DM with product value, campaign fit, deliverables, and a lightweight ask.

发送个性化邮件或私信，说明产品价值、合作匹配点、交付内容和轻量下一步。

5 Negotiate 议价

Push back once when quote is high. Recalculate estimated CPM and log final proposed terms.

报价偏高时至少议价一次，重新计算预估 CPM，并记录最终合作条件。

6 Approve 审批

Get internal approval on creator, content direction, cost, and expected performance.

对达人、内容方向、预算和预期表现进行内部审批。

7 Contract 签约

Confirm flat fee or flat + bonus, legal name, address, deliverables, timeline, and revision rights.

确认固定费用或固定费用加奖金、法律姓名、地址、交付内容、时间线和修改轮次。

8 Content Review 内容审核

Require script or concept before launch. Review for clarity, authenticity, and brand accuracy.

上线前必须有脚本或内容概念，重点审核表达清晰度、真实性和品牌准确性。

9 Launch And Track 上线与追踪

Paste post link into tracker. Pull 24h, 72h, and 7-day views, engagement, comments, and signups.

上线后将链接录入追踪系统，抓取 24 小时、72 小时、7 天播放、互动、评论和注册数据。

10 Review And Renew 复盘与续约

Compare actual vs estimated CPM, view achievement, conversion, and decide renew, retest, or pause.

比较实际与预估 CPM、播放达成率和转化，决定续约、换角度复测或暂停合作。

Outreach Sequence

建联沟通节奏

D0: Personalized opener D0：个性化开场

Reference a specific relevant post and explain why the creator fits the product story.

引用对方具体且相关的内容，并说明为什么这个达人适合产品叙事。

D1-D2: Campaign details D1-D2：合作细节

Share deliverables, timeline, revision process, and ask for rate card or expected pricing.

说明交付内容、时间线、修改流程，并询问报价或合作价格预期。

After quote: Negotiate once 收到报价后：至少议价一次

Anchor on expected views and CPM, then propose a fair flat fee or flat + bonus structure.

基于预期播放和 CPM 进行沟通，提出合理的固定费用或固定费用加奖金结构。

Before launch: Script required 上线前：必须有脚本

No script or concept, no collaboration. This protects brand accuracy and creator alignment.

没有脚本或内容概念就不进入合作执行，以保证品牌表达准确和达人方向一致。

Operating philosophy / 工作方法论

Influencer Marketing as a Performance System

把红人营销当作可复盘的增长系统

I manage influencer marketing as a performance system: starting with creator quality and audience fit, negotiating from expected CPM, requiring clear content review, and closing the loop with actual CPM, view achievement, signups, comment quality, and renewal decisions.

我会把红人营销管理成一个可衡量、可复盘、可迭代的增长系统：从达人质量和受众匹配开始，用预估 CPM 支撑议价，通过清晰的内容审核降低执行风险，并用实际 CPM、播放达成率、注册、评论质量和续约决策完成闭环。
