Influencer Marketing Operating Playbook

红人营销流程手册

A practical workflow for sourcing, evaluating, contracting, launching, tracking, and renewing creators in a U.S. growth team.

一套适用于美国增长团队的红人营销工作流，覆盖达人筛选、评估、建联、签约、上线、数据追踪与长期合作。

01

North Star And Funnel

核心目标与增长漏斗

Influencer marketing should be managed as a repeatable growth system, not a one-off creator list.

红人营销不应该只是一次性的达人名单，而应该被管理成一个可复用、可追踪、可优化的增长系统。

Target Audience 目标受众 U.S. college students, campus communities, creator-led trust 美国大学生、校园社群、由创作者建立的信任
Creator Quality 达人质量 View median, school depth, comment quality, posting cadence 播放中位数、校园渗透、评论质量、发布频率
Cost Efficiency 成本效率 Estimated CPM before deal, actual CPM after launch 合作前预估 CPM，上线后复盘实际 CPM
Business Outcome 业务结果 Signups, conversion quality, reusable creator partnerships 注册、转化质量、可长期复用的达人资产

02

Creator Evaluation Scorecard

达人评估打分卡

Follower count is a weak first gate. The stronger question is whether the creator can repeatedly reach the right students at an efficient cost.

粉丝量不是最可靠的第一筛选标准。更关键的问题是：这个达人能否以合理成本持续触达正确的学生人群。

30% 14-day view median 14 天播放中位数

Use recent median views to reduce volatility. Minimum consideration threshold: 50k median views.

用近期播放中位数降低爆款波动影响。建议进入考虑池的最低门槛为 5 万中位播放。

20% School signal 校园信号

Campus content, school in bio, visible student life, or strong school community authority.

包括校园内容、简介里的学校信息、学生生活场景，或在特定校园社群中的真实影响力。

15% Comment quality 评论质量

Look for real student comments, campus references, and conversation quality over vanity engagement.

优先看真实学生评论、校园语境和互动质量，而不是只看表层点赞互动。

15% Content fit 内容匹配度

Prioritize vlogs, POVs, campus stories, lifestyle narratives, and authentic on-camera presence.

优先选择 vlog、POV、校园故事、生活方式叙事，以及有真实镜头表现力的创作者。

10% Posting cadence 发布频率

Recent consistency matters because stale creators are harder to forecast and activate.

近期稳定更新很重要，因为长期不活跃的达人更难预测表现，也更难被有效激活。

10% Estimated CPM 预估 CPM

Formula: proposed price / view median * 1,000. Target below $20 unless content quality justifies an exception.

公式：报价 / 播放中位数 * 1000。目标低于 20 美元，除非内容质量特别强。

03

Operating Principles

操作原则

These principles keep influencer marketing disciplined, measurable, and scalable across campaigns.

这些原则用于保证红人营销不是靠感觉执行，而是可衡量、可复盘、可规模化地推进。

01 Quality over follower count 质量优先于粉丝量

Prioritize recent view performance, content fit, campus signal, and real comment quality.

优先看近期播放表现、内容匹配、校园信号和真实评论质量。

02 CPM before commitment 确认合作前先算 CPM

Use expected CPM to guide negotiation before a creator enters approval or contract stage.

在达人进入审批或签约前，用预估 CPM 支撑议价和预算判断。

03 Script before launch 上线前必须有脚本

Require a script or concept review to protect accuracy while preserving creator authenticity.

通过脚本或内容概念审核保证表达准确，同时保留创作者真实风格。

04 Post link as source of truth 发布链接作为数据源

Once content goes live, the post link triggers tracking, reporting, and campaign learnings.

内容上线后，以发布链接作为追踪、复盘和经验沉淀的核心数据入口。

05 Renewal is a performance decision 续约由表现决定

Long-term partnerships should be based on actual CPM, signups, view achievement, and comment quality.

长期合作应基于实际 CPM、注册、播放达成率和评论质量，而不是主观印象。

04

Decision Gates And Quality Controls

决策门槛与质量控制

Clear gates reduce wasted outreach, inconsistent pricing, delayed content review, and weak post-campaign learnings.

明确的决策门槛可以减少无效建联、报价混乱、内容审核延误和复盘沉淀不足的问题。

05

Workflow Map

完整流程地图

Each stage has a clear owner, decision gate, and data artifact. This makes the process scalable across teammates.

每个阶段都需要明确负责人、决策门槛和沉淀数据资产，这样流程才能跨团队复制和规模化。

1 Source 达人来源

Find creators from TikTok, Instagram, creator marketplaces, campus networks, and competitor mentions.

从 TikTok、Instagram、达人平台、校园网络和竞品提及中寻找候选达人。

2 Screen 初筛

Evaluate view median, school signal, comments, cadence, and content style before contacting.

建联前先评估播放中位数、校园信号、评论质量、发布频率和内容风格。

3 Duplicate Check 重复检查

Search the creator CRM and internal network before outreach to avoid messy double-contact.

在 CRM 和内部人脉中检查是否已联系，避免多人重复触达同一达人。

4 Outreach 建联

Send personalized email or DM with product value, campaign fit, deliverables, and a lightweight ask.

发送个性化邮件或私信，说明产品价值、合作匹配点、交付内容和轻量下一步。

5 Negotiate 议价

Push back once when quote is high. Recalculate estimated CPM and log final proposed terms.

报价偏高时至少议价一次，重新计算预估 CPM，并记录最终合作条件。

6 Approve 审批

Get internal approval on creator, content direction, cost, and expected performance.

对达人、内容方向、预算和预期表现进行内部审批。

7 Contract 签约

Confirm flat fee or flat + bonus, legal name, address, deliverables, timeline, and revision rights.

确认固定费用或固定费用加奖金、法律姓名、地址、交付内容、时间线和修改轮次。

8 Content Review 内容审核

Require script or concept before launch. Review for clarity, authenticity, and brand accuracy.

上线前必须有脚本或内容概念，重点审核表达清晰度、真实性和品牌准确性。

9 Launch And Track 上线与追踪

Paste post link into tracker. Pull 24h, 72h, and 7-day views, engagement, comments, and signups.

上线后将链接录入追踪系统，抓取 24 小时、72 小时、7 天播放、互动、评论和注册数据。

10 Review And Renew 复盘与续约

Compare actual vs estimated CPM, view achievement, conversion, and decide renew, retest, or pause.

比较实际与预估 CPM、播放达成率和转化，决定续约、换角度复测或暂停合作。

08

Outreach Sequence

建联沟通节奏

D0: Personalized opener D0：个性化开场

Reference a specific campus post and explain why the creator fits the product story.

引用对方具体的校园内容，并说明为什么这个达人适合产品叙事。

D1-D2: Campaign details D1-D2：合作细节

Share deliverables, timeline, revision process, and ask for rate card or expected pricing.

说明交付内容、时间线、修改流程，并询问报价或合作价格预期。

After quote: Negotiate once 收到报价后：至少议价一次

Anchor on expected views and CPM, then propose a fair flat fee or flat + bonus structure.

基于预期播放和 CPM 进行沟通，提出合理的固定费用或固定费用加奖金结构。

Before launch: Script required 上线前：必须有脚本

No script or concept, no collaboration. This protects brand accuracy and creator alignment.

没有脚本或内容概念就不进入合作执行，以保证品牌表达准确和达人方向一致。

Operating philosophy / 工作方法论

Influencer Marketing as a Performance System

把红人营销当作可复盘的增长系统

I manage influencer marketing as a performance system: starting with creator quality and audience fit, negotiating from expected CPM, requiring clear content review, and closing the loop with actual CPM, view achievement, signups, comment quality, and renewal decisions.

我会把红人营销管理成一个可衡量、可复盘、可迭代的增长系统：从达人质量和受众匹配开始，用预估 CPM 支撑议价，通过清晰的内容审核降低执行风险，并用实际 CPM、播放达成率、注册、评论质量和续约决策完成闭环。
